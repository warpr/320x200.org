welcome

Welcome, this is the 4k source compo page, a 4k compo organized by the regulars in #demoscene (freenode irc) - the opensource demoscene channel.

NOTE: the compo is over, results on the results page.

You can compete with any old 4k compo at parties, so we've decided to do things a little differently for this compo. The 4k size limit is for the source code of your entry - NOT the executable.

NOTE: there are no prizes, just fame and glory.

For questions, comments and flames: please visit #demoscene on freenode irc or send mail to warp (i'm a bit slow with email though, please try #demoscene first :)
