submit

Before you submit any entry, please read the rules carefully.

deadline

The deadline for submissions is friday 2003/03/14, 18:00 CET. (yes, the compo has ended, check the results page to see the entries :).

submitting

To submit an entry, prepare a .zip file or tarball (.tar.gz or .tar.bz2) with the following contents:

  • the source code for your entry
  • any Makefiles, project workspaces, batch files and shell scripts you use to build your entry
  • a file_id.diz is appreciated, but not required.
  • any other info files you wish to include.

NOTE: DO NOT SUBMIT ANY EXECUTABLES.

With all that out of the way, email your entries to fourk@frob.nl.

notes

If you need/want specific compiler switches to get the best perfomance out of your entry, please include a seperate .txt file with build instructions).

If I cannot build your entry you risk the chance of being disqualified (presumably because it's not portable). I will contact you by email or on #demoscene (freenode irc) if I have trouble building your entry - so you might want to submit your entry a little early to make sure you have enough time to fix any trouble should this occur.
