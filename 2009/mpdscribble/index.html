mpdscribble

mpdscribble is a music player daemon client which submits information about tracks being played to audioscrobbler.

I originally wrote mpdscribble as I was not entirely happy with mpdscrobbler. However, I am no langer maintaining it. Max Kellermann has taken over development, you will find his versions on the mpd wiki. Old versions are archived here.
