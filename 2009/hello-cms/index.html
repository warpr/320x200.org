Hello! CMS

Hello! CMS is a content management system designed for personal homepages. It allows a single person to easily edit any part of a website without the hassle of backend/frontend seperation used by larger content management systems or the inflexible nature of blogging applications.

I am no longer maintaining it, but the most recent versions have been archived here.
