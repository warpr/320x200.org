Welcome!

You've reached the homepage of bliss, a dutch demo group formed by the founding members of thaumaturge (which is now officially deceased).

As a demo group we strive to create electronic works of art called demos. All our releases will be released as Free Software, because we think that the Free Software Foundation's GPL has made the world a better place.

If you like IRC, please visit #demoscene on the openprojects irc network, or perhaps you prefer to meet us in person at a demo party, we try to visit most parties in or close to the netherlands.

-- warp
