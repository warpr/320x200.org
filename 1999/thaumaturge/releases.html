Preliminary information

Most of the files which are available for download in this section are executable programs for DOS. These demonstrations should work on any 486 or pentium with VGA and a Super VGA monitor or better. Some weird video modes might not work on your configuration. Also, to get any kind of sound you will need a Gravis Ultrasound.
