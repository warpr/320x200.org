Future Plans

We are currently migrating from our birth environment to the new environments which have come across our paths. That is, we used to make procedural intel x86 assembly code for the MS-Dos pseudo operating system and we're moving to object oriented platform independent C++ code for 32-bit operating systems (Unix, Windows).

When we're ready we'll resume our quest to create a production (demo or intro) which will be shown on a big screen at a dutch demo party during the compo and which will not get last in said compo.

Some of our members are working on side projects, most of these exist only in the mind of it's conceiver, some however are actually being worked on.
