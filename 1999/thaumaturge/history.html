History

Just a short timeline which lists most releases and group changes. For more information on Thaumaturge history please contact Warp.

1994

  • Tharsis & Warp found Thaumaturge, Shako joins the next day.
  • Our first release is a tune by Shako which he calls This SUXXXXXXXXX!!!!!!!!.
  • Pakjesavond is released on the 5th of december.

1995

  • Spark joins.
  • "Beter Laat Dan Nooit" is released, which is our 1 year anniversary intro.
  • Aap/Acme and Tharsis got it into their heads to organize a so-called flippo-party - which was to be held at the monthly SuccesS meeting in Utrecht (the Netherlands). Somehow Shako got involved which resulted in a us creating an invitation intro for the event. This "fliptro" features art by Sunday/Mistery and music by Harlequin/SuccesS.
  • Shortly after SiH'95 Shako leaves Thaumaturge to work on other projects
  • At Bizarre 1995 we attempted to get our intro "smurfig" on the big screen, but failed. We released it anyway.

1996

  • "Enjoy These Moments" is released, this sounddemo includes a small 2 player game which is quite enjoyable for a while.
  • Tharsis surprised Warp by creating an intro for him to celebrate Warp's 20th birthday. (This intro made it onto the cover cd of a french magazine called pc collecter, this particular issue had an article about the demo scene).

1997

  • Mugshot joins
  • Due to a compiler bug our intro "Nothing You Can Do" crashed when it was shown on the big screen at X'97 Takeover (A bug-fixed version still hasn't been released).

1998

  • Picaroon joins

1999

  • First Thaumaturge release in 2 years, the nycd Linux port.

2000

  • Thaumaturge is dead, as most of its members were inactive. (Tharsis and Warp will continue their demoscene efforts in bliss).
