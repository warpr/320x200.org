After a visit to El Hema, I decided to give the world famous dutch demoscene brand “Limp Ninja” the same treatment.

The El Hema exhibition was born out of a project by the Khatt foundation to create new arabic typefaces, I would have used these new typefaces for this image, but they are not freely available. So, I instead used whatever safari came up with on my mac.
