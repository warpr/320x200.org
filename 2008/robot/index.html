PIXELART ROBOT ICONS

I spent a few hours this morning trying to pixel a robot head in 32x32 and failing miserably. I was never the best graphician in the world, but it seems my pixeling skills have deteriorated even further.

This was triggered by a thread on the binaerpilot forum called "PIXELART ROBOT ICONS ONLY", the subject referring to the avatars used.

Eventually I came up with the one displayed here. Pixeled in Deluxe Paint II, which runs surprisingly well in DOSBox.
